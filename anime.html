SYNOPSIS

Naruto is a Japanese anime series based on Masashi Kishimoto's manga of the same name. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a dream of becoming the strongest ninja and the leader of his village, known as the Hokage. Naruto is an orphan, and he harbors the powerful Nine-Tails Fox, a fearsome creature sealed within him at birth.
